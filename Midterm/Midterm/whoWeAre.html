Car Ads is...

Car Ads is a company that lets advertisements play from a rear window of a car, while the driver earns ad revenue. We are devoted to helping companies we believe in spread their word, and helping drivers everywhere make some extra money to pay for gas by going no extra miles.

The Vision

We envision sitting in rush hour traffic on a daily commute, while the car in front of you tells you about new local restaurants or upcoming concerts in your area, while you are getting paid for advertisements on the back of your car for someone else to watch.
