Bio:

Former First Lady Michelle LaVaughn Robison Obama, from Chicago, Illinois, and mother of Sasha and Malia, was more than a just a First Lady while her husband, Barack Obama, served as President. This Princeton and Harvard law graduate worked at a law firm and as the Associate Dean of Student Services. She has spoken at the 2012, 2014 and 2016 Democratic National Conventions. She advocated for proper nutrition, poverty awareness, womens rights and proper education for all.
