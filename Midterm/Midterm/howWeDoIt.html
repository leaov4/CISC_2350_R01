Technology:

We replace the window of your vehicle with one of our windows that doubles as a screen on one side. The window is still perfectly transparent.

In terms of what ads play on your vehicle, this is determined by our team here, and the areas in which you drive. We play relevant ads to your location, which we track through Google Maps.
